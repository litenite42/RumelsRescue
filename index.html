You were out for a pleasant ride with your best friend in the world; your pet Rumel The Lemur. The two of you were having quite the joyful occasion when a large truck swerved out in front of you causing you to veer off road.

You came to pinned by the body of your motorcycle and can only look on as a pair of flannel covered arms swoop into view and take grasp of Rumel; laying unconcious but a few meters away!

Horrified, you struggle against the weight on your legs to no avail. VRROOOMMM!! The truck spits and belches dirt all over as it roars away.

A sudden strength comes over you and you're able to leverage the vehicle off of you. A look at the thing tells you it's going to be a bumpy ride, but Rumel needs you!

Keep pressing W/↑/LMB/Tapping your screen to try and catch up to the thieves! How long will you make it?
